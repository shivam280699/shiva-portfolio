About Me

Hello! I'm Shiva, a PhD scholar at Central University of Kerala working on heavy ion fusion studies. My research focuses on understanding nuclear reactions, particularly quasifission processes in superheavy element formation.

I completed my M.Sc. in Nuclear Physics with 76.8% and have been actively contributing to the scientific community through publications and conference presentations. My work involves sophisticated Monte Carlo simulations, data analysis using CERN ROOT, and developing machine learning models for nuclear data interpretation.

I'm passionate about bridging theoretical physics with computational methods, and I actively contribute to the ROOT forum community, helping fellow researchers solve complex analysis challenges.

Here are some technologies I work with:

  • CERN ROOT & C++
  • Python & Machine Learning
  • Geant4 Simulation
  • Monte Carlo Methods
  • Neural Networks
  • Linux & Bash Scripting
